Instructions-PROTECTOR

When you see these two symbols at the start of your turn, you will play the role of PROTECTOR. The symbols also tell you the value of each colored word on that turn. At the end of each turn you will see how many bombs are left in the container.

Remember, this image means that you are on a LOW-VALUE turn where each correct response will remove only bomb.

Remember, this image means that you are on a HIGH-VALUE turn where each correct response will remove bombs.
