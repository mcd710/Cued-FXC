Rules of the game

Congratulations! You have mastered the basic operation in this game. Now we will introduce the rules.

Rule 1
You own a pile of gems at your home, which is the equivalent of $ of bonus payment.

However, there exist bombs in this game. Bombs can destroy your gems.

Rule 2
There are two types of containers in the game. Some of the containers are empty. The other containers are filled with bombs.

Rule 3
On each turn in this game, you will play one of 2 roles: either the COLLECTOR or the PROTECTOR.
As the COLLECTOR, you will try to create gems.
As the PROTECTOR, you will try to prevent your gems at your home from being destroyed by bombs.
