Let's COLLECT AND PROTECT gems!

You are about to start the real game.

As a reminder, at the beginning of the game, you own a pile of gems at your home.

There will be rounds of the game. In each round, you will perform on several turns.

NOTICE: In each round, the containers on will be shipped to your home. You don't know which turns will be selected so you should provide as many correct responses as possible on each turn.

Once all shipments are finished, the gems and bombs in the received containers will be added to the gem pile at your home. Each bomb added to your pile will detonate and destroy one gem.

Your remaining gems in your pile will be converted to real money at the end of the game.
