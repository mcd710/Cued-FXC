Instructions


Let's practice planting each type of seed! When you see a fruit on the screen press the button to plant the seed.

You need to respond while the fruit is on the screen, which will only be 2 seconds.
After each response, you will be given feedback about your performance. Do your best to pay attention to the task. If you accidentally hold down a key, you will get a brief time out.

To plant each seed, please use your...
Left middle finger to press D to plant
Left index finger to press F to plant
Right index finger to press J to plant
Right middle finger to press K to plant
