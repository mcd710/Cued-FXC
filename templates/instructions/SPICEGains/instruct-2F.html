In garden plots you will see fruits with labels on them. These labels will be the names of the fruits. Sometimes the fruit will be correctly labeled and the label will match the fruit image and other times the wrong label will be on the fruit.

Importantly, you should continue to press the key to plant the seed for the IMAGE of the Fruit. You should not press a key based on the label.
