Instructions-COLLECTOR

When you play the role of COLLECTOR, you will create gems.

At the start of each turn, there is a new empty container to fill.

You will create some amount of gems every time you provide correct response to a colored word you saw earlier. However, the amount of gems you create per correct response depends on the value of colored words on that turn.

When you are on a LOW-VALUE turn, each correct response on that turn creates only gem.
When you are on a HIGH-VALUE turn, each correct response on that turn creates gems.

NOTICE: You will neither create nor lose any gems when you give an incorrect response.
