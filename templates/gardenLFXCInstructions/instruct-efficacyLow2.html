Instructions

Every turn, you will continue to see a fruit tree image. It will ALWAYS be a PERFORMANCE Tree.

Every turn there are also RANDOM Trees, but you will never see a fruit image for a RANDOM Tree because they do NOT require you to water them to produce fruit.

RANDOM Trees produce fruit which you can collect at the end of the turn randomly and not based on your watering performance during the turn.

However, you will not know if you can collect fruit from the RANDOM Trees until the end of a turn.

If you are collecting fruit from the RANDOM Trees at the end of the turn you will see this image:

This image means the amount of fruit you collected and the number of coins you earned was NOT based on how well you watered the PERFORMANCE Trees during the turn.
