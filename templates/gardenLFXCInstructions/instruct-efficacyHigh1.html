Instructions

Every turn, you will continue to see a fruit tree image.

Every turn there are PERFORMANCE Trees, and you will ALWAYS see a fruit image for a PERFORMANCE Trees because they do require you to water them to produce fruit.

PERFORMANCE Trees produce fruit which you can collect at the end of the turn based on your watering performance during the turn.

However, you will not know if you can collect fruit from the PERFORMANCE Trees until the end of a turn.

If you are collecting fruit from the PERFORMANCE trees at the end of the turn you will see this image:

This image means the amount of fruit you collected and the number of coins you earned was based on how well you watered the PERFORMANCE Trees during the turn.
