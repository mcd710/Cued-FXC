Instructions

Now the real game will start where you can earn bonus money for collecting fruit and earning coins.

After each turn you find out which type of tree you can collect fruit from.

The fruit you collect is worth coins.

You will see which tree you collected fruit from and how many coins you earned at the end of each turn.

At the end of the game, we will choose 6 of these turns at random and pay you an additional bonus based on the total amount of coins you collected across those 6 turns.
