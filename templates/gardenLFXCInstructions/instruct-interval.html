Instructions-Perform on turns

The game will be broken up into turns. Each turn you will have a fixed amount of time (about 7 seconds), so the faster you respond, the more fruit trees you can water.

Each turn will start with a fruit image and the label. Once you press the key to water the fruit a new fruit and label will appear. This will keep happening until the time for that turn runs out.

You will continue doing the same task. Remember, you should respond only to the PICTURE of the fruit and NOT based on the label.
