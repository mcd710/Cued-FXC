Now you will do several turns.

Every turn when you see a fruit tree image, it will ONLY ever be a PERFORMANCE Tree.

After each turn you will see which type of trees you can collect fruit from and how many coins you earned.

In this practice, you will ONLY collect fruit from RANDOM TREES at the end of the turn.
