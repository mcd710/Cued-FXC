Instructions

Every so often after a turn you will be asked:

How much do you think the number of fruits you collect and coins you earn depends on your watering performance?

If you move the slider to the left this means you think the number of fruits you collect and coins you earn does not at all depend on your performance watering the PERFORMANCE Trees during the past few turns.

If you move the slider to the right this means you think the number of fruits you collect and coins you earn depends completely on your performance watering the PERFORMANCE Trees during the past few turns.
